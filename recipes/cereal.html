Cereal

This is the cereal you will be making

Cereal is a versatile and convenient breakfast staple enjoyed by people of all ages. Typically made from grains such as wheat, oats, corn, or rice, cereal can be found in a variety of forms, including flakes, puffs, and clusters. Often fortified with vitamins and minerals, cereal provides a quick and nutritious start to the day. Many cereals are also flavored with ingredients like honey, chocolate, or fruit, catering to different tastes and preferences. The simplicity and speed of preparation make cereal an ideal choice for busy mornings.

To make your own basic cereal at home, start by choosing a base grain such as rolled oats or puffed rice. Preheat your oven to 300°F. In a large bowl, mix the grains with a sweetener like honey or maple syrup, and add a pinch of salt for flavor balance. Spread the mixture evenly on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally until it turns golden brown. Let it cool completely before transferring it to an airtight container. Enjoy your homemade cereal with milk, yogurt, or fresh fruit for a delicious and wholesome breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Paprika
  • Maple syrup
  • Salt
  • Nuts (optional)

Cooking Steps

  • Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C).
  • Mix Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine 3 cups of rolled oats, 1/4 cup of honey, 1/4 cup of maple syrup, and a pinch of salt. Optionally, you can add 1/2 cup of chopped nuts for extra crunch.
  • Spread on Baking Sheet: Spread the mixture evenly on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  • Bake the Cereal: Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes to ensure even baking, until the cereal turns golden brown.
  • Cool and Store: Remove from the oven and let the cereal cool completely. Once cooled, transfer it to an airtight container for storage. Enjoy your homemade cereal with milk, yogurt, or fresh fruit.
